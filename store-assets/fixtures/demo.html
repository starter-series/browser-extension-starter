Why a starter template

A good template gives you continuous integration, a release pipeline, and a tested settings flow on day one — so you can spend your time on the feature that makes your extension worth installing.

Tip: anything wrapped in a <mark> tag — like this highlighted phrase — gets painted by the content script using the color from your settings.

What you get

  • Manifest V3 for Chrome and Firefox from one source.
  • Jest unit tests and a permission audit in CI.
  • A one-command store-asset generator for screenshots.
